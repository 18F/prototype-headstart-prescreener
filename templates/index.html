Could your child be eligible for a Head Start program?

Any of these could make your child eligible:

Your income.
Your living situation.
If your child is in fostercare.
Your eligibility for other programs.

Important information to know:

Even if they are eligible, your child is not guaranteed a Head Start slot.
An interview is part of every Head Start application.
You will need to provide documents that verify your eligibility.
This website is not a final determination of eligibility for Head Start.

Take this pre-screener to find out if your child might be eligible.

An interview is an important part of the Head Start process. This web pre-screener will not take the place of an interview. It may help you decide to apply for Head Start.
